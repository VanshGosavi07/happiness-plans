About Happiness Plans

Your trusted partner in travel and event management since 2021.

Based in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, we specialize in creating personalized travel experiences and managing memorable events. Our team of dedicated professionals ensures every detail is perfect, from the initial planning to execution.
